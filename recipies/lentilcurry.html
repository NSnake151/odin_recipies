Lentil Curry

Description

Rich and wholesome, lentil curry combines earthy lentils with aromatic spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk for a creamy texture and robust flavor profile.
This vegan delight is packed with protein and nutrients, making it a nourishing choice for any mealtime.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Onion
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. Cook Onion.
  2. Combine Onion with Lentils and spices, cook.
  3. Serve with rice.

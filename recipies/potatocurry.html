Potato Curry

Description

A comforting dish with tender chunks of potatoes simmered in a fragrant blend of spices like cumin,
turmeric, and garam masala, creating a hearty and satisfying meal that pairs perfectly with fluffy rice or warm naan bread.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. Cook Onion.
  2. Combine Onion with Potatoes and spices, cook.
  3. Service with rice.
